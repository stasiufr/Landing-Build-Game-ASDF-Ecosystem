What is this?

An automated system that collects trading fees and burns tokens. That's it.

üí∏

Fees are collected

When people trade tokens on Pump.fun, creator fees are generated. These fees flow to the treasury wallet automatically.

üîÑ

Tokens are bought

Collected SOL is used to purchase $ASDF tokens from the open market. No promises about timing or amounts - just when thresholds are met.

üî•

Tokens are burned

Purchased tokens are sent to a burn address with no private key. Permanently removed from supply. Verifiable on Solscan.

The Process

Automated and verifiable

1

Trading happens

People buy/sell tokens. Pump.fun generates creator fees.

2

Fees collected

Daemon detects and collects fees to treasury wallet.

3

Buyback executed

When threshold met, SOL buys $ASDF on market.

4

Tokens burned

$ASDF sent to burn address. Supply reduced forever.

5

Holders win

No trading needed. Just hold and let the burns do the work.
